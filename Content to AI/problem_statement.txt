Understanding MTS  (Make To Stock)
Products are manufactured or purchased BEFORE customer demand.
Example: Make To Stock
Current Stock:
Wooden Chair = 100 Units Customer orders:
10 Wooden Chairs
Result
The system delivers directly from stock. No procurement needed.
(Make To Order)
Products are manufactured or purchased ONLY when a customer order arrives.
Example: Make To Order
Current Stock:
Wooden Table = 2 Units Customer orders:
10 Wooden Tables Shortage:
8 Units
